=============================

Today, I started a new project, but I got so tired and hyper-focused that I even forgot to talk about it in my Journal.
It's nothing extremely complex, but it's extremely necessary.
Initially, all my projects start as a solution to my own problems, but I always keep the code open and public in case someone is interested or has a similar problem.
Anyway... the project's name is Vault. It's an extremely small and lightweight "mini launcher CLI" for games and programs, being extremely useful for non-Steam games and old Wine games, but completely usable in modern games, as long as the user knows how to prepare their own prefix after Vault creates it.

* vault: https://github.com/sakaki91/vault
