Active Projects:
Arcade.Wrapper-Linux.
This wrapper adapts and manages "everything" that TeknoParrot does, but on Linux. For games emulated with emulators such as Triforce=Dolphin, Namco System 147/246=Play, and RPCS3, native emulators are prioritized to remove as many layers of abstraction as possible. If this is not possible, the emulator (TeknoParrot/OpenParrot, among others) is used, translated via Wine/UMU.
